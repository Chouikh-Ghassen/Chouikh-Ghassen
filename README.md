# 💫 About Me:
I'm an AI & Software Engineer with a background in Electrical Engineering, specializing in the full journey from model architecture to physical deployment. Whether it's training a deep learning model, explaining its decisions with XAI, or squeezing it into a microcontroller at 134 microseconds latency, I work across the entire stack.<br><br>At FZI Research Center in Karlsruhe, I spent nearly two years building explainability pipelines for autonomous driving systems, applying LRP, Grad-CAM, and D-RISE to CNNs and object detectors like YOLOv5 and Faster R-CNN to uncover bias and failure cases before they became safety issues. My Bachelor's thesis took that applied mindset further: I built an end-to-end ML pipeline deployed on FPGA and Arduino, achieving 88% model size reduction with under 6% accuracy loss through Int8 quantization. More recently, I've expanded into LLM engineering, building RAG systems with LangChain and GPT-4o, and completing professional certifications in multi-agent workflows and LoRA fine-tuning.<br><br>I'm now looking to join a team where AI isn't just a prototype, it ships. Ideally in autonomous systems, industrial AI, or applied GenAI, where I can bring both the hardware instincts and the LLM toolkit to problems that actually matter.<br><br>Let's connect.
